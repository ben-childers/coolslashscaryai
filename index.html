About

We believe the only way to ensure AI is equitably realized is to ensure that as many voices contribute to its growth — not just five billionaires in Silicon Valley.

Cool Slash Scary AI brings together nonprofit leaders, social entrepreneurs, and civic technologists to shape AI with the communities most affected by it.

Who it’s for

Mission-driven leaders and doers across nonprofits, philanthropy, civic tech, advocacy, and local communities who want to engage AI with care, clarity, and collective action.

Mission

We are a decentralized event platform that brings communities together to discuss and act on the impact of AI in their lives.

What we stand for

  • AI shaped by communities, not dictated by hype cycles.
  • Clear governance and shared standards that earn trust.
  • Learning that is open, repeatable, and locally led.

How we work

  1. Convene local leaders around concrete challenges.
  2. Document insights and publish open impact summaries.
  3. Support organizers with lightweight, human-centered infrastructure.
